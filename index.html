Development · 22Evolution

Partner With
22Evolution

22Evolution is the land development and investor partnership arm of 22Builds. We acquire, entitle, and develop residential communities across Alabama — and we partner with select investors who share our conviction in the Gulf Coast market.

If you are an investor seeking exposure to Alabama residential real estate with an experienced operator, we welcome a conversation.

01

Land Acquisition

22Builds identifies and acquires residential development sites across Alabama with precision — analyzing market conditions, infrastructure readiness, lot yield potential, and absorption rate before committing to any parcel.

02

Due Diligence

We conduct comprehensive due diligence on every opportunity: title review, environmental screening, zoning analysis, utility access, and cost-to-develop modeling. Investors receive full transparency before capital is committed.

03

Development & Build

22Evolution — our residential development arm — manages site entitlement, infrastructure development, and vertical construction. Turn-key communities built to the same standard as our individual custom homes.

04

Market Strategy

We develop in markets where demand structurally exceeds supply. Alabama's Gulf Coast and surrounding corridors offer strong in-migration, employment growth, and persistent housing deficits — creating durable return opportunities.

Community — IMPACT 22

Building Homes
Is Only
Part of It.

Ryan Anderson didn't just build a company. He built IMPACT 22 — the community and social impact arm of 22Builds — because he knows that the communities around us define the world we live in.

IMPACT 22 focuses on youth development, safe environments, workforce readiness, and community investment across Alabama. Every home we build is connected to a broader purpose — creating opportunity for the next generation.

The discipline that builds great homes builds great communities. At 22Builds, these two commitments are inseparable.

◈

Youth Empowerment

IMPACT 22 invests in Alabama's next generation — providing mentorship, programming, and resources to young people in underserved communities across the Gulf Coast.

◈

Safe Environments

Creating safe, positive environments for youth to develop character, discipline, and confidence. We believe where you grow up should never determine how far you can go.

◈

Workforce Readiness

Skilled trades are the backbone of residential development. IMPACT 22 supports workforce readiness programs that connect young people to real career pathways in construction and real estate.

◈

Community Development

Every home we build strengthens a neighborhood. Every neighborhood we invest in strengthens a community. Impact is not a side project — it is the mission behind the mission.

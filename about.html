Founder

Ryan Anderson

Former Professional Athlete · Residential Developer · Founder, 22Builds

Ryan Anderson's path from the professional arena to residential development is not as unlikely as it sounds. The same principles that defined his athletic career — discipline, accountability, precision under pressure, and a relentless pursuit of excellence — are the exact principles required to build exceptional homes.

After his professional career, Ryan saw a clear gap in Alabama's residential market: families who wanted quality homes and a trustworthy process weren't finding both. He founded 22Builds to close that gap. Every home we build is a direct expression of Ryan's commitment to doing it right — no shortcuts, no compromises.

Ryan remains personally involved in every 22Builds project — not as a figurehead, but as the person accountable for delivering what we promised. That's how it will always be.

"I built this company the same way I built my career — by outworking the competition and refusing to cut corners."
Ryan Anderson, Founder

Community — IMPACT 22

Beyond The
Build.

IMPACT 22 is the community and social impact arm of 22Builds. Ryan Anderson founded IMPACT 22 with the conviction that building homes is only part of the responsibility of building in a community.

Through IMPACT 22, 22Builds invests in youth empowerment programs, safe environments for young people, workforce readiness in the skilled trades, and community development across Alabama.

Ryan believes the discipline that builds great homes can also build great communities — and that both are worth pursuing with the same intensity.

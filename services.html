Custom Home Build

Turn-key, start to finish.

22Builds handles every phase of your new construction — from pre-construction planning through keys-in-hand delivery. Ryan Anderson built this company on the principle that your builder should be accountable for the whole project, not just the parts that are easy.

We manage land preparation, permitting, scheduling, subcontractors, materials procurement, and quality control under one roof. You get weekly updates, clear timelines, and a team that treats your build the way they'd want someone to treat theirs.

  • Site preparation and land clearing
  • Permit management and code compliance
  • Full subcontractor coordination
  • Weekly client communication and budget reporting
  • Quality inspections at every phase
  • Keys-in-hand delivery with walkthrough

Renovation

Great bones. New life.

Not every great home starts from bare earth. Sometimes the best investment is transforming what you already own. 22Builds approaches renovation with the same discipline we bring to new construction — because shortcuts in renovation show up later, and we're not interested in later problems.

We specialize in comprehensive renovations: structural, mechanical, finish, and everything in between. If you want a cosmetic refresh, we're not the right team. If you want a renovation that's built to last, we are.

  • Kitchen and bath transformation
  • Room additions and expansions
  • Structural modifications
  • Full mechanical and electrical updates
  • 22Builds finish standards throughout
  • No shortcuts — built to new construction standard

Design Services

Your vision, made real.

Great homes don't happen by accident. They start with intentional decisions about how spaces connect, how light moves, how finishes age. Our design team works alongside you from the beginning — not as a last step.

We coordinate everything from floor plan customization and exterior elevation selection to interior material choices, lighting, and fixture specification. You won't be handed a catalog and told to figure it out. We walk you through every decision and tell you what works.

  • Floor plan customization and modification
  • Exterior elevation and material selection
  • Interior finish specification
  • Kitchen and bath design
  • Lighting design and fixture coordination
  • Full coordination with build team — no handoff gap

Floor Plan Selection

Four plans. One standard.

22Builds maintains four proven residential floor plans — each designed for real life in Alabama. The Alyssa, The Houston, The Wallace, and the Chattanooga "22" represent different footprints and lifestyles, but every one of them is built to the same standard.

Select a plan as-is, or work with our design team to customize it. Either way, you start from a foundation that's been tested and refined across more than 22 homes in Alabama. No guessing. No reinventing the wheel.

  • The Alyssa — 4 bed / 3.5 bath / 3,230 sf / Modern Craftsman
  • The Houston — 4 bed / 3 bath / 2,320 sf / Traditional
  • The Wallace — 4 bed / 2 bath / 2,050 sf / Farmhouse
  • Chattanooga "22" — 4 bed / 3.5 bath / 3,300 sf / 1.5-Story Signature
  • All plans fully customizable
  • Full documentation and specifications provided

Project Management

Accountability at every phase.

Ryan Anderson runs each 22Builds project personally — not as a figurehead, but as the person responsible for delivering what was promised. That means you always know who to call, and that person always knows exactly where your project stands.

We provide structured project management with milestone tracking, budget transparency, and direct communication. If there's a problem, you hear it from us first — with a plan to solve it. We don't hide behind vague timelines or change-order surprises.

  • Dedicated project lead on every build
  • Milestone-based schedule tracking
  • Weekly written progress updates
  • Transparent budget reporting and change order process
  • Subcontractor coordination and accountability
  • Post-completion punch list and warranty support

Land Development

From raw land to ready lot.

22Evolution — our land acquisition and development arm — identifies undervalued residential land across Alabama, performs comprehensive due diligence, and develops it into shovel-ready residential lots. We understand Alabama land because we've built on it: the soil, the water tables, the permitting requirements, the communities.

Whether you've already found your lot or need help identifying the right piece of land, 22Evolution has the expertise and relationships to get it right from the start.

  • Land identification and acquisition
  • Zoning and entitlement analysis
  • Environmental and title due diligence
  • Site clearing and grading
  • Infrastructure and utilities coordination
  • Investor partnership opportunities via 22Evolution
